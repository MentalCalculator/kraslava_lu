Sightseeing objects

The Slum of Sauleskalns

An apartment building abandoned by fate since the 1990s. Nice Soviet heritage.

Adrese: Sauleskalns, Kombulu parish, Kraslava district

Recreation Complex "Sauleskalns"

In this recreation complex, you can rent a boat or a raft, and you can swim in Dridzis. After active rest, it is possible to enjoy a meal in the restaurant "Sauleskalns" or in an outdoor cafe.

Adrese: Sauleskalns, Kombulu parish, Kraslava district

Lake Dridzis

The ridge is located in a subglacial bed. The lake is stretched in the west-east direction, very narrow at the eastern end, and splits into two branches on the western side. The bed is sandy, pebbly, silt in the creeks. The depth makes the water appear dark. The coasts are heavily indented, steep in some places.
