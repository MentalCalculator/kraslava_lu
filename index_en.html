A Long, but Interesting Adventure

It's a route with interesting sightseeing places from Kraslava to University of Latvia (LU).

Stoppoints of the Route Kraslava - University of Latvia

1. Kraslava

One of the most beautiful Latgalian pearls near Daugava. It's unique with multinational population, architecture of old houses and picturesque views.

2. Dridzis

It's the deepest lake in Baltics. Its deepth is around 65 meters.

3. Aglona

A small and cute town in widely Latgalian expanses. A significant destination of Catholic pilgrimages.

4. Madona

The highest town in Latvia. One of the most cleanest and safest Latvian cities.

5. Ligatne

A cute alternative to Sigulda with high rocks, old manors and own soul. The former place of cult of Latvian paper.

6. Sigulda

One of the most popular destinations in Latvia. Beautiful nature, wide possibilities of active life, highly developed infrastructure.

7. Riga

Welcome to our wonderful capital city!
